    EXPERIENCES

    FESTIVALS

    Kerala is host to a plethora of traditional festivals that take place throughout the year. A wide number of regions and communities are involved in these celebrations. There are huge processions and massive displays on the street with crowds thronging to soak in the pomp and splendour.

    FOOD

    Spices have always played an integral part of Kerala's history. Our food is an example of the very same and we have offerings for people from all over the world.The cuisine here caters to vegetarians and non-vegetarians alike, featuring a rich tapestry of flavors and culinary traditions that leave a lasting impression.

    ARTFORMS

    Kerala has birthed and nurtured a plethora of art forms over a thousand years. Our shores have watched them evolve and flourish in various shapes and forms. From the enchanting Kathakali dance to the rhythmic beats of traditional percussion, the cultural tapestry of Kerala is a living testament to its rich heritage.

    Explore the Districts of Kerala

    Discover the unique charm of its districts, from the tranquil hill stations of Wayanad and Idukki to the vibrant cities of Kochi and Thiruvananthapuram. Experience the pristine beaches of Kannur and Kovalam, the historic sites of Thrissur and Palakkad, and the wildlife sanctuaries of Periyar and Silent Valley.
